AYLO Core
Privacy Policy

AYLO Core is the Super App that evolved from Fayz. It includes spiritual tools, Ihsan Garden learning modules, community profiles, video services, messaging, wallet features, gifts, moderation and future service modules.

Last updated: May 3, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how AYLO Core (the "App") collects, uses, stores and protects information. The former Fayz prayer app is now one module inside AYLO Core, so this policy covers the whole Super App.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for third-party advertising tracking.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account data — email address, user ID, username, display name, profile details, authentication/session metadata and account status.
  • Profile and community content — avatar, cover photo, bio, posts, videos, comments, likes, views, follows, reports, appeals and moderation history.
  • Messages and service requests — conversations, attachments, support requests, handyman/service inquiries and related delivery/status data.
  • Location data — used for prayer times, Qibla direction and location-based features when you grant permission. Some features may store an approximate location or coordinates needed to provide the service.
  • Wallet, gifts and purchases — AYLO balance, internal token ledger, gift/tip records, StoreKit product IDs, transaction identifiers and purchase status.
  • Device and app data — app language, locale, notification tokens, device permissions, crash/debug logs and basic technical events needed to keep the App reliable and safe.
  • Fayz spiritual module data — prayer calculation settings, Quran reading progress, daily tracker progress, downloaded audio/cache preferences and notification settings.

3. How We Use Information

  • To create and secure your account.
  • To show profiles, posts, videos, rankings, factions and service listings.
  • To enable messaging, comments, reports, blocks, appeals and admin moderation.
  • To calculate prayer times, Qibla direction and spiritual progress in Fayz.
  • To process AYLO gifts, wallet ledger updates and Apple in-app purchase state.
  • To prevent abuse, spam, fraud, unsafe content and unauthorized access.
  • To provide support and improve reliability, performance and safety.

4. Payments, AYLO Tokens and Gifts

Digital items, AYLO token packs, Ihsan Garden plans, badges, gifts and other subscriptions, when enabled, are processed through Apple StoreKit / In-App Purchase on iOS. AYLO tokens are internal digital items for use inside the App. They are not cryptocurrency, securities, cash, banking products or investment products.

We store purchase and ledger records so balances, gifts and moderation/audit trails can be restored and verified.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

  • Supabase — authentication, database, storage and server-side data.
  • Cloudflare — media/video delivery and storage where configured.
  • Vercel or other hosting providers — API and web hosting.
  • Apple — App Store, StoreKit purchases, subscriptions and device services.
  • Expo / Apple Push Notification services — push notification delivery.
  • Quran.com / EveryAyah and similar content sources — Quran text, fonts, recitations or related Islamic content where used by Fayz.

6. Permissions

  • Location — prayer times, Qibla and location-aware features.
  • Camera and photo library — profile images, posts, videos and uploads you choose.
  • Microphone — video recording or audio features you choose.
  • Notifications — prayer reminders, account activity, messages and service updates.
  • Motion/compass sensors — Qibla direction and related spiritual tools.

7. Children and Age

AYLO Core contains community, messaging, video, wallet and moderation features. Users must be old enough to use these features under applicable law and the App Store age rating for the current release. Parents or guardians should supervise minors. We do not knowingly sell children's data or use it for advertising tracking.

8. Data Retention, Deletion and Safety

We keep account, content, transaction, safety and moderation records for as long as needed to provide the App, protect users, comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes. You can request account deletion or data support through the App or by contacting us.

Some deleted content may remain in backups, audit logs or moderation records for a limited period where needed for safety, fraud prevention or legal compliance.

9. International Processing

AYLO Core is a cross-border app. Your data may be processed in countries where our service providers operate. We use reasonable safeguards appropriate to the data and service.

10. Changes

We may update this policy as AYLO Core grows. Material changes will be reflected in the App, on this page or in release updates.
